Good evening everyone, this is the Oregon Emergency Net, club call W7OEN.
The Oregon Emergency Net is a Public Service and Friendship Net with participants in and out of the State of Oregon. Visitors are always welcome.

This Net meets daily at 6:00pm on or about 3,980 kHz.
The OEN provides a consistent meeting time for use during an emergency, and at other times for making contacts, and sharing member updates.
We will assist with emergency situations and informal messages whenever possible.
Please do not interrupt the regular roll call to check early or late but instead wait for any one of the several breaks throughout the roll.
