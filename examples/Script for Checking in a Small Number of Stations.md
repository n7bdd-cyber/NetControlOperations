# Script for Checking in a Small Number of Stations

This is <your call sign, your first name>, subnet control operator for this <deployment or exercise> in the <your region name(s)> region. This subnet is operating on <subnet frequency> without a repeater. Make sure your transceiver is not using a repeater offset as an offset will keep us from hearing your transmissions.  

Our format during this deployment is the following:

- First, we’ll check in operators. This check-in process may include some relays. During this process please make notes on how well you can hear other stations.
- After the check-in process is complete, we will conduct formal communications.
- When the deployment period comes into an end, I will then ask you to check out.
- After all stations have checked out, I will close the net.

**[Preface to checking stations in]**

In a minute, I will start checking stations in. I will check in stations in our region by tactical call sign rather than FCC call sign. Your tactical call sign should be based on your team name followed by a digit. For example, South Heights 1. Be prepared to change the digit after your team name if someone else checks in with your first choice before you do. After I recognize you, give your FCC call sign and say the word “Out”.  

As you hear other stations check in, please log their tactical call signs and how well you can hear them. If you hear a station that I don’t acknowledge, please wait until I ask for relays before telling me who I missed.

**[Start of Check in]**

Let’s start the check-ins. After I recognize you, give your FCC call sign and say the word “Out”. Please come now with your tactical call sign.

[Record each station on your [Stations Participating Stations Log](https://volunteerpdx.net/images/a/aa/Stations_Participating_Log.pdf) and acknowledge each station as they check in. Expect operators to acknowledge your acknowledgement by giving their FCC call sign, which you can also log if you like.]

If anyone heard a station that I did not acknowledge, please say the proword Relay.

[Handle any relays.]

Are there any missed or late check-ins? Please come now with your tactical call sign.

[Acknowledge, as above.]

[To cover the possibility that you cannot hear some of the operators, ask for a relay using [Procedure for When Net Control Operator Cannot Hear Some Operators](https://volunteerpdx.net/index.php/Procedure_for_When_Net_Control_Operator_Cannot_Hear_Some_Operators)]

[To cover the possibility that some operators cannot hear you use [Procedure for When Some Operators Cannot Hear Net Control Operator](https://volunteerpdx.net/index.php/Procedure_for_When_Some_Operators_Cannot_Hear_Net_Control_Operator).]

Thanks everyone for checking in.

**[End of Check in]**
