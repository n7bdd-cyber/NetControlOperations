CLACKAMAS COUNTY, OREGON
Amateur Radio Emergency Service
CARES Net Script
Good (Morning/Afternoon/Evening). This is (your call sign). My name is (your first name), and I am net control for KD7ZDO, the
Clackamas Amateur Radio Emergency Service net, better known as the CARES Net.
NET CONTROL INSTRUCTION: Read the line enclosed below only when conducting the Sunday evening net.
This net meets each Sunday evening at 1900 hours, local time, on this frequency.
The purpose of this net is to distribute messages and information to members of CARES and other net participants. Emergency traffic will
be handled at any time, by saying “break” to interrupt the net.
This is a directed net. All stations are asked to please remain on this frequency unless excused. We will call stations the following order:
EchoLink, Clackamas County, Multnomah County, Washington County, and then visitors. Stations will be called by the first letter of their
call-sign suffix. All check-ins are welcome. Please use proper ITU phonetics when checking in.
Do we have any emergency traffic at this time? Please reply with your call.
NET CONTROL INSTRUCTION: Once all Emergency traffic has been listed, call for any stations on frequency that
can handle the listed traffic
